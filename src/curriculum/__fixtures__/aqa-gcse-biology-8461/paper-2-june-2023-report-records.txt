[misconception]
locator: General / Particular problems
topicRef: 4.5.3.3
description: Has weak or fragmented understanding of kidney function and the role of the kidney in homeostasis.
triggerPatterns: kidneys remove all water || kidneys only store urine || kidneys make urea
correctionGuidance: The kidneys filter the blood, selectively reabsorb useful substances and water, and regulate water and ion balance while urea is excreted in urine.
severity: 3

[misconception]
locator: General / Particular problems
topicRef: 4.6.1.2
description: Confuses meiosis with mitosis or cannot use meiosis to explain genetic variation.
triggerPatterns: meiosis makes identical cells || meiosis has one division only || meiosis is for growth and repair
correctionGuidance: Meiosis halves the chromosome number, produces genetically different gametes, and underpins variation in sexual reproduction.
